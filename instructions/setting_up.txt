1. Setup the ros master and ros client IP_address (IP address of the raspberry pi):
	In Your Raspberry pi:
		-open the terminal (ctrl + alt + T)
		-type in the following command:
			$ vim ~/.bashrc
		This will open the bashrc file. Go to the bottom of the file. Find the following lines:
			
			export ROS_MASTER_URI=http://192.168.0.106:11311  [This is the ros master IP address i.e. IP address of your raspberry pi. FInd your raspberry pi's IP address and change it]
			export ROS_IP=192.168.0.106 [This is the IP address of the client device]
			
		- save the file
